This course explores the application of artificial intelligence and deep learning techniques in biomedical imaging, including image reconstruction, enhancement, analysis, and diagnosis.

- Prepared and delivered tutorials on specific topics such as Implicit Neural Representations and Deep Learning for Image Reconstruction
- Assisted students with course projects involving biomedical image reconstruction using deep learning techniques
- Conducted lab sessions on microscopy image enhancement and aberration correction techniques
